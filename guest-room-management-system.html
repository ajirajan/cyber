GUEST ROOM MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Business Services

A GRMS integrates various in-room technologies and services into a centralized platform, allowing hotel staff and guests to control and monitor room conditions, preferences, and services. It’s designed to enhance comfort, reduce energy consumption, and streamline hotel operations.

Core Features:

Smart Room Controls

Guests can adjust lighting, temperature, curtains, and entertainment systems via touch panels or mobile apps.

Energy Management

Automatically turns off lights and HVAC when rooms are unoccupied, reducing utility costs.

Occupancy Detection

Uses sensors to detect guest presence and trigger appropriate settings.

Service Requests

Allows guests to request housekeeping, room service, or maintenance digitally.

Integration with PMS & BMS

Syncs with Property Management Systems and Building Management Systems for seamless operations.

Access Control

Supports key card or mobile access, often integrated with systems like Assa Abloy, Saflok & Salto.
